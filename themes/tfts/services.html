HVAC

TFTS install & service HVAC systems. We provide HVAC maintenanve for the retail, commercial, residentials and public sectors. We offer very competitive preventive maintenance contracts ranging from smalll clients to large multi-building, industrial sites. HVAC preventive maintenance not only helps to ensure that equipment is operating correctly and safely, but it also serves to help maintain unit performance and system efficiency as well as prolonging unit life expectancy.

We can plan maintenance contracts, specific to your requirements, from filter cleaning to a comprehensive maintenance program.

Our in-house engineers are qualified in the maintenance and service procedures for all different types of HVAC systems.

Area of expertise

  • Duct cleaning
  • Odor Control
  • Air Balancing
  • Chilled water systems
  • Package air-condition units
  • HVAC preventive maintenance
  • HVAC installation & maintenance
  • FCU, AHU installation and maintenance
  • Installing humidifiers and dehumidifiers
  • Maintenance of duct layouts & related equipment (VCD, VAD etc)
  • AC, Ventilations & Air Filtration system installation & maintenance
